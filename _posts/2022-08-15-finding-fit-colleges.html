Resources from this Session

  • Big Future College Search  – CollegeBoard run database with a variety of filters that allow you to filter schools by a variety of criteria
  • College Greenlight  – database with schools with lots of resources specifically for first-generation college students
  • Colleges that Change Lives  – database of Liberal Arts Colleges
  • ScholarMatcher  – database/map of high–graduation–rate schools (check the CollegePoint Schools box to find top colleges with 70%+ graduation rates)
  • I–m First  – database similar to College Greenlight with schools that are committed to supporting first–generation students
  • QuestBridge Partner Schools  – list of QuestBridge partner schools; although you aren–t that sure if you want to apply for the QuestBridge scholarship, look and see if there are a lot of schools on your list that are also QuestBridge partner schools
