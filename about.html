About Me

Hi there! My name is Caleb and I’m so excited to get to meet you! I’m currently a junior at UC Berkeley studying Public Health and Data Science from the LA area. I love reading, running, and listening to music (especially Sara Bareilles!), and this is my second time now as a Matriculate Advising Fellow. About Matriculate

Matriculate is a 501(c)(3) non–profit organization providing free, virtual near–peer advising to students across the country to empower high–achieving, low–income high school students to make the leap to some of the nation’s best colleges and universities.
Matriculate was established in the fall of 2014. In the spring of 2015, we launched a pilot by recruiting, selecting, and training 70 college student Advising Fellows from Columbia, Howard, Princeton, and Yale Universities. Advising Fellows began working with high–achieving, low–income high school students around the country, from places like Carson City, Nevada and Stillwater, Oklahoma. Today there are more than 800 Advising Fellows across 13 partner colleges and universities advancing Matriculate’s mission of expanding access to quality higher eduation to America’s best students regardless of their socio–economic status or background—giving them the opportunity to change the trajectory of their own lives and the lives of their families.
